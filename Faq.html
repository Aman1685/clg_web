Frequently Asked Questions

▾What courses does the college offer?

We offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Computer Science, AI & ML, Cyber Security, and more.

▾How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions portal or contact the admissions office for assistance.

▾What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

Eligibility criteria vary by course. Generally, you need to have completed your higher secondary education with relevant subjects.

▾Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, the college offers merit-based and need-based scholarships. Please contact the financial aid office for details.

▾How can I contact the college administration?

▾You can reach us via the Contact Us page or call the administration office during working hours.

▾What facilities are available on campus?

Our campus includes modern labs, libraries, sports facilities, and student lounges to support your learning and extracurricular activities.

▾What is the campus library's operating hours?

The campus library is open from 8 AM to 10 PM on weekdays and 9 AM to 5 PM on weekends.

▾Is there on-campus housing available?

Yes, the college offers on-campus dormitories for students with various room options.

▾Are internships provided through the college?

We have partnerships with several companies to provide internship opportunities for students.

▾What extracurricular activities are available?

Students can participate in clubs, sports teams, cultural events, and volunteer programs.

▾How do I access the student portal?

The student portal can be accessed through the college website using your student credentials.

Have a question? Ask us!
