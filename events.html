Event / Activities

Upcoming Events

Academic Calendar

Annual functions and events are organized by the college. The college hosts various programs throughout the year, including workshops, seminars, guest lectures, and cultural activities. These events provide students with opportunities to enhance their skills, network with professionals, and showcase their talents.

All students are encouraged to participate in these events to gain practical experience and contribute to the vibrant campus life.

Athletic events are also organized to promote physical fitness and sportsmanship among students. These include inter-college competitions, sports meets, and recreational activities. Participation in such events promotes a healthy lifestyle and fosters teamwork.
