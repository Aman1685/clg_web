Engineering Department

Undergraduate Programs

Welcome to the Engineering Department

The Engineering Department at S.I.E.T Panchkula is dedicated to providing cutting-edge education in emerging technologies. Our undergraduate programs focus on artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, robotics, and automation, preparing students for careers in research, industry, and entrepreneurship.

Why Choose Engineering at S.I.E.T?

  • Industry-Focused Curriculum – Courses designed to meet the demands of future tech industries.
  • Advanced Labs & Research Centers – AI labs, cybersecurity training rooms, and robotics workshops.
  • Internship & Placement Support – Partnerships with top companies for hands-on training.
  • Expert Faculty & Mentors – Learn from professionals with research and industry expertise.
  • Student Clubs & Tech Events – Participate in hackathons, innovation challenges, and conferences.
  • Scholarships & Financial Aid – Merit-based scholarships and financial support for deserving students.

Undergraduate Programs Offered

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) – AI & Machine Learning

    Focus: Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Data Science, NLP, and Predictive Analytics.

    Labs: AI Research Lab, Data Analytics Lab, and Machine Learning Lab.

    Career Paths: AI Engineer, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Research Scientist.

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) – Cyber Security

    Focus: Ethical Hacking, Network Security, Cryptography, Digital Forensics.

    Labs: Cybersecurity Training Lab, Ethical Hacking Lab, and Digital Forensics Lab.

    Career Paths: Cybersecurity Analyst, Ethical Hacker, Network Security Engineer.

  • Robotics & Automation

    Focus: Industrial Robotics, IoT, Autonomous Systems, Control Systems, and AI in Robotics.

    Labs: Robotics & Automation Lab, Embedded Systems Lab, IoT Lab.

    Career Paths: Robotics Engineer, Automation Specialist, IoT Developer, Control System Engineer.

Our Facilities & Resources

  • High-Tech Laboratories – AI & ML labs, cybersecurity simulation centers, and robotics workshops.
  • Smart Classrooms – Interactive digital learning with AI-powered teaching tools.
  • Library & Digital Learning – Access to research journals, AI models, and cybersecurity tools.
  • Innovation Hub & Incubation Centers – Support for startup ideas and R&D projects
